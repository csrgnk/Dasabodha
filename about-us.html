About Us

Our website is dedicated to sharing the teachings of Sri Ram Das Swami through the Dasabodha Grantha.

The Dasabodha Grantha is a revered spiritual text that contains 20 Dashakams (chapters), and each Dashakam has 10 Samasams (verses), totaling 200 Samasams. Each verse provides practical guidance for living a spiritual and ethical life.

All of our posts present these teachings in Telugu lyrics, making the wisdom of Dasabodha Grantha accessible to Telugu-speaking devotees.

We regularly update the site with new posts, and while the content may be released daily, all published posts provide fast and easy access to the knowledge and guidance of God.
